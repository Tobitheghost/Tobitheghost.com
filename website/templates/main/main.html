About Me

My Name is Tobi and I live in Kansas City, Missouri (Go CHEIFS!) in the United States. While I have hardly been outside my State, let alone my Country, I have always loved exploring technologies and cultures. An easy way to accomplish this interest was through art. I graduated from an Art School and have been winning awards for Drawing or Poetry or Graphic Design from a young age.

While not having to much money growing up, learning to create art on a computer was easier since there was less materials needed. I learned Graphic Design, Photo Editing and more, jumping from interest to interest before getting a "Building a Web Site For Dummies" book for a birthday, and thus started my Software Development obsession.
